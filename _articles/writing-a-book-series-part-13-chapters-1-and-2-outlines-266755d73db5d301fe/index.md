**Chapter 1: Why is sleep important?**

Hook: Story about how Daylight Savings Time affects people's health.

Thesis: To demonstrate how important sleep is by listing facts about what happens to you when you don't get enough sleep.

Supporting content: 

*   Effects on the body
*   Effects on the mind and brain
*   Effects on relationships

Story: Traveling across the country and experiencing a three-hour time difference on a regular basis

Reader's key takeaway: Not getting enough sleep = bad and motivated to learn more about how to improve sleep.

Segue: Not getting enough sleep is bad, but you can fix it. Start with something simple like the place where you sleep every night--the Boudoir!

**Chapter 2: Sleep Environment**

Hook: Describe my bedroom before I optimized it

Thesis: Provide considerations for optimizing the sleep environment

Supporting content:

*   What should and should not be located in the bedroom
*   The activities that should and should not take place in a bed
*   Mattresses/pillows
*   Significant others/pets
*   Controlling light/sound/temperature

Story: I know a couple of people who fall asleep with the TV on; A needy/kneady cat

Reader's key takeaway: Proper sleep requires a proper environment

Segue: Now that you have improved your sleep environment, the next step is to optimize your evening routine that is 1-2 hours before night night.
